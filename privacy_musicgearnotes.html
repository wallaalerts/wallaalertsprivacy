1. Introduction

Welcome to Music Gear Notes ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

Music Gear Notes is a simple, offline-first application. We collect only minimal data necessary to operate and improve the App:

  • User Content: Notes, images, and related content you create are stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Data: Basic, anonymous information about how the App is used (such as screen views or app opens) collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash Data: Technical information related to app crashes and performance, collected via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Advertising Data: Non-personal identifiers (such as advertising IDs) may be used to display ads.

3. How We Collect Information

Information is collected in the following ways:

  • Automatically through Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics
  • Through third-party advertising services used to display ads

4. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Understand general app usage
  • Monitor app stability and fix crashes
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Display advertisements

5. Data Storage

All notes and images created within Music Gear Notes are stored locally on your device. We do not sync, upload, or back up your data to any cloud services.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal data. Limited data may be shared with:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics) for app monitoring and analytics
  • Advertising partners for the purpose of displaying ads
  • Authorities if required by law

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services such as Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and advertising networks. These services may collect information as governed by their respective privacy policies.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information collected through the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Local data security also depends on the security of your device.

9. User Control
