Privacy Policy for Walla Alerts App

Last updated: August 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Walla Alerts App ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email address (when you choose to log in)
  • Authentication Data: Encrypted password (when you create an account)
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including search queries and notification preferences

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you when you create an account or use the App
  • Automatically as you navigate through the App (usage data)

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Notify you about new products matching your searches
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you and respond to your inquiries
  • Ensure the security of the App

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., Firebase for authentication)
  • Advertising partners (e.g., AdMob for serving ads)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For user authentication and data storage
  • Google Sign-In: As an optional login method
  • AdMob: For serving advertisements
  • Google Maps API: For displaying maps and location-based information. Note that our app does not send any location data to Google or any other third parties.

Please refer to the privacy policies of these services for more information on how they handle your data.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

13. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union (EU) and European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This means:

  • We process your data lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
  • We collect and process data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes.
  • We only collect and process data that is necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes.
  • We ensure the data is accurate and kept up to date.
  • We keep personal data in a form that permits identification for no longer than necessary.
  • We process data in a manner that ensures appropriate security.

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • The right to erasure of your personal data
  • The right to restrict processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing of your personal data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights or for any GDPR-related inquiries, please contact us at walla.alerts.app@gmail.com.
