Privacy Policy for Toilet Finder Barcelona

Last updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Toilet Finder Barcelona ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

Toilet Finder Barcelona is designed to prioritize your privacy. We collect minimal data:

  • Advertising Data: AdMob (Google) may collect information for displaying personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Location Data (Optional): The app may access your real-time location only if you grant permission. This data is used to display your current position on the map. We do not store or transmit this location data to our servers or third parties.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • When you grant location permissions to display your location on the map.
  • Automatically through AdMob’s SDK for advertising purposes.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use information solely for the following purposes:

  • To display your location on the map (if enabled).
  • To serve ads via AdMob.
  • To ensure the functionality and performance of the app.

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not store, sell, or share your personal information. However, data collected by AdMob may be shared with Google and their partners for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. Please review Google’s Privacy Policy for more details.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • AdMob (Google): For displaying ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, location (if enabled), and other usage data for advertising and analytics purposes.

We do not control how AdMob processes data. Please see Google Ad Policies for more information.

7. Data Security

We do not store or process any user data on our servers. Any data handled by AdMob is encrypted in transit using Transport Layer Security (TLS).

8. User Rights

You have the right to control certain types of data processing:

  • You may revoke location access at any time in your device settings.
  • You can manage AdMob’s personalized advertising preferences via Google’s ad settings.

9. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
